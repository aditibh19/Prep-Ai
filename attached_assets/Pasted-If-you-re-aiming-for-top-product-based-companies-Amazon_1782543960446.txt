# 🌟 Features That Will Really Make Recruiters Remember Your Project

If you have limited time, prioritize these unique capabilities:

1. **Adaptive AI Interviewer** – adjusts question difficulty based on previous answers.
2. **Human-like Cross Questioning** – asks follow-up questions naturally instead of a fixed list.
3. **Resume-Aware Interviews** – generates questions from the user's uploaded resume.
4. **Persistent Interview Memory** – remembers previous sessions and focuses on weak areas.
5. **Personalized Readiness Score** – combines DSA progress, interview performance, resume quality, and study consistency into a single metric.
6. **Company-Specific AI Mentor** – changes advice and interview style based on the selected company.
7. **Weekly AI Progress Report** – summarizes achievements, weaknesses, and next week's study plan.

These features create a coherent product that feels like a real SaaS platform rather than a collection of disconnected AI tools. More importantly, they give you excellent topics to discuss in interviews: system design, AI integration, real-time communication, database modeling, and product thinking.
